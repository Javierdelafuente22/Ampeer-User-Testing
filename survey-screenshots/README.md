## Tips

- Cards render at roughly `9 / 16` (portrait phone). Match that aspect ratio
  for the cleanest result.
- 540 × 960 minimum is fine. Higher is better — the browser scales down.
- Keep the filenames exactly as listed; they are referenced from
  `survey/Stage2.jsx` and `survey/Stage3.jsx`.
